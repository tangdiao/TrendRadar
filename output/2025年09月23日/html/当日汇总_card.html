Huawei plans to overcome these limitations by scaling chip connectivity and memory access speeds, with the Ascend 970 projected to achieve 4 terabit-per-second interconnect speeds by 2028. While challenges remain, including poor yields and restricted access to advanced equipment, Huawei remains confident in its strategy to advance China’s AI industry through clustering and networking technologies.
